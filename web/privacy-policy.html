Privacy Policy

How Chakame handles your personal information and data

Last Updated: January 2025

This privacy policy explains how Chakame handles your personal information and data when you use our application.

Data Collection

Chakame collects minimal data to provide its services:

  • Your favorite poems (stored locally on your device)
  • App settings and preferences (stored locally)
  • Notification preferences

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide personalized poetry recommendations
  • Save your favorite poems for easy access
  • Remember your app preferences
  • Send daily poem notifications if enabled

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to third parties. The app connects to the Ganjoor API only to fetch poems, but no personal information is sent with these requests.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Favorites are saved in local storage
  • Settings are stored in app preferences
  • No cloud storage or external databases are used

You can delete all data by clearing the app's cache or uninstalling the application.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View all stored data in the app settings
  • Delete specific favorites or clear all data
  • Export your favorites for backup
  • Disable notifications at any time

Since all data is stored locally, you have complete ownership and control.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the app's support channels or visit our website.
